Meet Cephra: The Future of ERP

Powered by the Octopus One orchestration engine.

Cephra is the first product coming from OctopusOne — an intelligent, industry-specific ERP platform built for service-based businesses. Unlike generic enterprise software, Cephra arrives pre-configured for the way your industry actually works. Each edition is purpose-built for a specific vertical — field service, safety consulting, hospitality — so your team goes live faster and adopts the system naturally.

The flagship edition, Cephra Field Service, connects every role in a field service organization — from the sales rep quoting the job, to the technician on site, to the client approving changes through a branded portal. Site knowledge preserved. Change orders automated. Clients informed in real time.

CephraMind, OctopusOne's agentic intelligence plugin, can be added to any Cephra edition — turning your ERP into an autonomous operating system that dispatches, predicts, and acts on your behalf.

CephraMind does not replace human judgment — it eliminates the administrative burden that prevents skilled people from focusing on the work that matters.
